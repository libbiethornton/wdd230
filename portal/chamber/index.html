Memorial Day Flags

The Logan Chamber of Commerce is supplying local businesses with an American flag to put outside on Memorial Day to honor our troops. If you would like to be included in this, or if you would like to volunteer with the distribution of the flags reach out to Lisa at (435)834-2314.

Independence Day Parade

The Logan Bank is putting on the annual Independence Day parade on July 4th. If you would like to volunteer or sign up for a spot in the parade, please stop by the bank during their business hours of 8-5 Monday through Friday.

Summer Kick-Off Pool Party

The Logan Aquatic Center is hosting a summer kick-off pool party on June 5. All passes will be 20% off for the day and season passes purchased that day will receive a 10% discount.

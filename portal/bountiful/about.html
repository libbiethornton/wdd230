About Us

Bountiful Foods was started in 1963 by a young farmer and his wife, Mack and Marlene Miller. Their goal was to provide fresh produce to their community. People continued to love the quality of their produce, so it kept expanding. In over 60 years of business we now supply fresh produce to much of Southern California from Los Angeles to San Diego. As we've grown our goal has remained the same, to provide high qualtiy good tasting produced to our community. We remain a family owned company. Visit us in store in Carlsbad or have one of our food trucks bring it straight to you. We appreciate your business and love serving the people of Southern California.

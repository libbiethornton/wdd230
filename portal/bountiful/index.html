Get to Know our Coast!

The southern coast of California is los of sandy beaches and much warmer than the northern coast. You can find many hills, mountains, and even islands along it. The entire California coast is sometimes referred to as the Golden Coast. Some of the common commodities produced from California are dairy products, grapes, almonds, strawberries, pistachios, lettuce, tomatoes, and walnuts.

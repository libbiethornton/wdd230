Target Audience

The target audience for this website is the local business owners. That includes mostly men and woman around the ages of 30-60. This includes members of the chamber of commerce.

Persona 1

Sheryl is the owner of the local flower shop. She has just recently been in business for the past three years and is excited that her business is doing well. She is looking for ways to get involved in the community and support other local business owners as she has been supported the last several years.

Persona 2

Steve has been a member of the Logan Chamber of Commerce for over 20 years. He owns the local candy shop which was handed down to him from his grandfather. He enjoys getting to support other local businesses and the community that he feels with them.

Scenario 1

Sheryl is looking for ways to get more involved in the community. She goes to the Logan Chamber of Commerce website and sees that there is a meeting being held this Thursday. She is excited to attend.

Scenario 2

Steve has a question about one of the deals at a local business. He hops onto the Logan chamber of commerce website and navigates to the page talking about recent deals and promotions by the local businesses. He sees the answer to his question.

Scenario 3

Sheryl is looking for a way to promote her fairly new business in the Logan area. She goes to the city's chamber of commerce website and sees that there is a place for local business owners to put in their information about deals or promotions on the website. She enters in the information and is happy to see good results from her efforts.

Scenario 4

Steve remembers that there is a meeting for the chamber of commerce, but cannot seem to remember where it's at this time. He goes onto the website and checks to see where it is located.

Scenario 5

Sheryl wants to become apart of the Logan Chamber of Commerce, but she's not sure how. She goes to the website and sees that there is a spot for her to fill out information about herself to then be considered to be a member. She fills the information out and is excited to hear back about it.
